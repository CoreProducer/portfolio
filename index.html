Core

Producer / Mix Engineer

Core — a reflection of ourselves at the center of all being.

I'm a music producer studying a Bachelor of Music (maj. Music Production) at JMC Academy, Australia. My music reflects my inner experience by blending my childhood electronic inspirations with organic life and analogue hardware, recorded piano, and resampling my daily experiences captured on a cassette recorder from the 90s. The reliance on inorganic technology needs to be balanced with the organic. The resulting amalgamation of my experience is what became Core.
